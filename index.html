About Me

I'm Tia, founder of CCC Services. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Human Resource Management and am CIPD Level 5 qualified, with professional experience at the CIPD, the global HR and L&D body, now with a growing presence in the UAE.

Whilst this experience shaped my understanding of workplace wellbeing and internal structures, CCC is not a HR service. It was designed as a distinct offering: confidential, discreet support that exists outside of internal company systems.

After moving away from corporate life due to my own experience with burnout, I began exploring more intuitive approaches to emotional support and launched an anonymous virtual wellness page. Originally created to share guidance and tools for navigating life pressures, I noticed a recurring theme: many people weren't seeking advice, but rather a space to be heard without judgment.

This led me to begin my coaching training, which I pursued in parallel, completing a Coaching for Business qualification with City University London. I'm now working towards the ICF Associate Certified Coach (ACC) credential via the Portfolio Pathway, with over 40 hours of ICF-accredited training completed to date.

CCC bridges the gap between internal workplace structures and traditional therapeutic routes by offering consistent, private conversations with the same trusted coach each time. This isn't therapy or HR. It's a protected space to release pressure, gain clarity, and feel supported. A modern wellbeing check-in, a call in confidence that slots gently into the working day, without the pressure to perform or present.
